ABOUT

Personal Background

I grew up around the Chicago suburbs in Illinois and now
I currently attend Illinois State University. Constantly
finding a new exciting hobby to take on has been a
consistent trend throughout the majority of my life. From
oil painting to nail artistry to playing guitar, I love trying
new creative outlets whenever I get the chance. Graphic
design is my passion and it pushes me everyday.

Professional Skills

At Illinois State University I am currently in the BFA
program for Graphic Design. This program hones in my
skills in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and many
other important programs. I also have taken classes in
video editing, photography, typography, color theory,
web design, and plan on taking many more throughout
the rest of my college experience at ISU.
